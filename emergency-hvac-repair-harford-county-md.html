Emergency HVAC repair in Harford County, MD

If your HVAC system stopped working, you have no heat, no AC, or a suspected safety issue, submit an emergency request to route your issue faster.

When to treat HVAC service as an emergency

No heat or no AC

Complete system failure, especially during extreme temperatures.

Burning smell or smoke

Turn the system off if safe to do so and request urgent service.

Leaks or frozen coils

Water issues can cause damage quickly—request help and document symptoms.

What to include in your emergency request

Provide your ZIP code, urgency = “Emergency,” and a short description of symptoms (no airflow, strange noises, leaking, odor). This improves routing and reduces back-and-forth.
